FAQS

What services do you offer?

We offer a range of interior decoration services, including space planning, color consultation, furniture selection, wall treatments, lighting design, and complete home or office makeovers. Whether you need a simple refresh or a full transformation, we've got you covered!

How do I get started with your services?

Getting started is easy! Simply contact us to schedule a consultation. We’ll discuss your vision, preferences, budget, and timeline to create a customized plan that suits your needs.

Can you work within my budget?

Absolutely! We tailor our designs to fit different budgets without compromising on quality and aesthetics. During our consultation, we’ll help you choose the best options within your price range.

How long does an interior decoration project take?

The timeline depends on the scope of the project. A simple room refresh may take a few days, while a full home or office makeover can take weeks. We always provide an estimated timeline before starting any project.

Do I need to buy new furniture, or can you work with what I have?

We can do both! If you have existing furniture that fits your space and style, we can incorporate it into the design. If needed, we also help select and source new furniture that complements the overall look.

Can you help with small spaces?

Yes! We specialize in maximizing space efficiency, ensuring that even the smallest areas are stylish, functional, and well-organized.

Do you provide custom design themes?

Yes, we create personalized design themes based on your preferences. Whether you prefer modern, minimalist, classic, or a fusion of styles, we’ll craft a unique space that reflects your personality.

What makes Glitterz_Exclusive different from others?

At Glitterz_Exclusive, we blend creativity, functionality, and attention to detail to create breathtaking spaces. Our personalized approach, commitment to quality, and passion for design set us apart.

How do I book a consultation?

You can book a consultation by reaching out to us via phone, email, or through our website. Let’s bring your dream space to life!

Do you handle both residential and commercial projects?

Yes! We specialize in designing stunning interiors for homes, offices, hotels, restaurants, and other commercial spaces.
